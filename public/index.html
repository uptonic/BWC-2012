Experience the BW Difference

Relax. We'll Take Care of You.

Our Lead Carpenter works on site all day, every day to make sure your custom remodel runs like clockwork and, despite construction, you return each day to a safe, clean home. BW Construction takes professionalism, open communication and integrity as seriously as our carpentry and project management skills.

We Believe Communication is Key.

Nobody likes surprises when it comes to a home remodeling project. Over the past 20+ years, we've gotten estimating down to a science. We share the scope, cost, schedule and building details with you upfront and we stick to them. The only surprise you'll experience is how amazing your home looks when we sweep our way out the door.

Shouldn't Dreams Be Fun?

We believe that if you're finally making your dream come true, you should enjoy the process. This is our passion, not just a job, and we're as excited about your home as you are! We have been building and remodeling homes in and around Boulder since 1989 and are proud to call our clients friends, long after the work is finished.

Your Vision, Built To Last.
